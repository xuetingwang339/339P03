Interests in Employment

Tuned Magazine falls under the lifestyle category, similar to a guide focusing on organic food and life. Each new edition will introduce readers to seasonal-based ingredients, offer recipes and encourage readers to create their own new recipes. Additionally, Tuned includes health information regarding ways to improve wellness through food. Furthermore, each issue of Tuned will have a brief article introducing an organic Canadian ranch or farm, inspiring readers to discover and explore unique ingredients or recipes from that region. The purpose of Tuned is to make organic living more appealing, helping to improve health while changing the environment for the better.
