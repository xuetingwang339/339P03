This book is a lifestyle of Denmark. Hygge is a Danish and Norwegian language used to express comfort and comfort, as well as health and satisfaction. I choose 8x9.5 for this book, which is the standard size for a hardcover book. The purpose of this book is to introduce this lifestyle to those who don't understand it. Moreover, the book provides a variety of ways to achieve this lifestyle. I want to design this book to convey this lifestyle to the reader, and I want the reader to think that the book is worth buying and reading. For this project, I got inspiration of classic European colors like light blue, grey which are not bright. Also, I searched some lifestyle book and learned from the layout and font choice. I want to create a clear visual hierarchy for reading. And unify all the spread to one style by the grid and color. Also, I will use some small elements to communicate the main content.

Tuned Magazine falls under the lifestyle category, similar to a guide focusing on organic food and life. Each new edition will introduce readers to seasonal-based ingredients, offer recipes and encourage readers to create their own new recipes. Additionally, Tuned includes health information regarding ways to improve wellness through food. Furthermore, each issue of Tuned will have a brief article introducing an organic Canadian ranch or farm, inspiring readers to discover and explore unique ingredients or recipes from that region. The purpose of Tuned is to make organic living more appealing, helping to improve health while changing the environment for the better.
