Liftstyle Magazine: Tuned

Cover and Spread for Lifestyle Magazine

Concept the Megazine

Tuned Magazine falls under the lifestyle category, similar to a guide focusing on organic food and life. Each new edition will introduce readers to seasonal-based ingredients, offer recipes and encourage readers to create their own new recipes. Additionally, Tuned includes health information regarding ways to improve wellness through food. .

Furthermore, each issue of Tuned will have a brief article introducing an organic Canadian ranch or farm, inspiring readers to discover and explore unique ingredients or recipes from that region. The purpose of Tuned is to make organic living more appealing, helping to improve health while changing the environment for the better

Process

Tuned's visual identity will be built upon a bold yet minimalist design with photography driven aesthetic. The logo is used Circular Bold and cooperates with " Book Antiqua." Using the line element to highlight the organic concept of Tuned. For each issue, Tuned will use minimalist organic food photography to be the cover image and use a larger size of the font to emphasize the cover story. The cover image looks show the magazine concept and fresh grapes full of life. The issue number will be put at the right side of the edge and can be seen when flipping through. For the cover line, I used a bold size to contrast with the background image, and a larger size text for the cover story. Other titles follow the grid system.

For the cover story, Tuned used clean negative space to catch the reader's eyes. I utilize the font" Electra LT Std" to be the body content, which is clear to read. The other bonus one, I put a video to show some article content. Video is a useful way to innovate reader.

Reflection

Through this work, I learned how to develop a reader for one magazine. The style of the magazine needs to be considered according to the audience, and the preferences of different income, age and gender will be different. Also, I learned how to reflect the uniqueness of the magazine by name, edition story and content.
